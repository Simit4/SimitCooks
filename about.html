Our Story

Welcome to Simit Cooks! We celebrate simple, home-style cooking with flavors from Nepal and beyond. Every recipe is crafted with passion.

Our Journey

2018

Started in Melbourne kitchens as a kitchen hand, gaining experience and learning the fundamentals of professional cooking.

2022

Became a professional chef in Melbourne, refining skills and mastering home-style and international recipes.

2025

Launched Simit’s Swaad website and YouTube channel to share easy, home-style Nepali recipes with audiences worldwide.

Our Vision

To make home cooking joyful, simple, and accessible to everyone, while celebrating the rich flavors of Nepal and beyond.
