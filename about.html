About Us

Welcome to Simit's Swaad! We bring the joy of simple, home-style cooking from Nepal to your kitchen. Every recipe is crafted with love, fresh ingredients, and a passion for sharing delicious meals.

From beginners to seasoned home cooks, our step-by-step recipes, tips, and tricks make cooking approachable, fun, and rewarding.

Our Journey

2018

Started in Melbourne kitchens as a kitchen hand, learning the ropes of professional cooking.

2021

Became a professional chef in Melbourne and started sharing recipes online through Simit’s Swaad.

2022

Created a YouTube channel to bring easy, home-style Nepali recipes to viewers worldwide.

Our Vision

To make home cooking joyful, simple, and accessible to everyone, while celebrating the rich flavors of Nepal and beyond.
