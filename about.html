About Us

Namaste! 🙏 Welcome to The Home Cookery — a space dedicated to celebrating the joy of simple, home-style cooking. I'm a Nepali chef based in Melbourne, passionate about sharing the rich flavors of Nepal and beyond.

With years of culinary experience and deep love for traditional recipes, I aim to make cooking approachable for everyone. From vibrant street foods to comforting curries, you'll find recipes that are easy, flavorful, and made with real ingredients.

The Home Cookery is not just a website — it’s a journey through taste, culture, and creativity in the kitchen. Whether you're cooking your first meal or exploring new cuisines, you'll find something delicious here.

Let’s bring the taste of home to every plate! 🍛🍜
